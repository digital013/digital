Introduction

App is an online community platform designed to connect mothers, enabling them to share their experiences and provide mutual support throughout various phases of their lives.

Problem Statement

The objective of the app is to drive user acquisition, increase awareness, and provide actionable insights to the CEO for informed decision-making.

Responsibilities

Our main responsibilities include:

  • Executing targeted advertising campaigns across various channels such as social media, search, and display networks.
  • Coordinating with design and development teams to ensure app store listings are optimized for maximum visibility and conversion.
  • Setting up and monitoring app analytics tools such as Firebase, Mixpanel, Google Analytics or Clevertap.
  • Generating regular reports on key performance metrics including downloads, engagement, retention, and revenue.
  • Presenting findings and recommendations to cross-functional teams and stakeholders.
  • Continuously tracking and measuring the impact of marketing initiatives on app performance.

Achievements

Here are some achievements of the project:

  • Successfully launched the app, achieving 1000 targeted downloads within the first month.
  • Increased in-app engagement by 25% through the introduction of gamified features.
  • Implemented customized app performance dashboards, increasing visibility and accessibility of key metrics.
  • Provided actionable insights leading to a 25% improvement in overall app performance metrics.
  • Improved app store conversion rate by 15% through iterative A/B testing.
  • Improved user retention rate by 30% through targeted push notification campaigns.
