Introduction

Top surgeons at affordable costs, who offer advanced procedures.

Problem Statement

Enhance visibility and drive targeted traffic to client websites through paid search campaigns, improve website conversion rates to maximize the effectiveness of digital marketing efforts

Responsibilities

Our main responsibilities include:

  • Conduct keyword research to identify high-performing search terms.
  • Develop compelling ad copy and creative assets.
  • Monitor campaign performance metrics such as clickthrough rate (CTR), conversion rate, and return on adspend (ROAS).
  • Implement A/B testing strategies to optimize ad performance.
  • Collaborate with the design team to create engaging landing pages.
  • Analyze user behavior through tools like Google Analytics and Clarity.
  • Collaborate with UX/UI designers to create intuitive and conversion-focused website experiences.
  • Utilize audience targeting tools to reach the most relevant demographics.
  • Monitor campaign performance and adjust targeting parameters to maximize ROI.

Achievements

Here are some achievements of the Firstcure project:

  • Implemented real-time reporting dashboards, improving data accessibility and decision-making efficiency.
  • Received positive feedback from clients/stakeholders for clear and actionable reporting insights.
  • Successfully lowered cost per acquisition (CPA) by 20% through audience optimization.
  • Increased CTR by 25% through ad copy refinement.
  • Achieved a 30% improvement in conversion rate through continuous optimization.
  • Increased the ROAS by 5X using paid marketing channels and still improving.
